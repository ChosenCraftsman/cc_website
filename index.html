About

Handcrafted work rooted in faith, built with integrity, and made to last generations.

Chosen Craftsman is a custom woodworking studio dedicated to creating pieces that carry meaning. Every joint, every surface, every design decision reflects an understanding that what we build with our hands can serve something greater than ourselves.

With a focus on specialty gifts, wedding gifts, and wall art, each project begins with a conversation — about story, about what each piece is to be for the people who will live with it.

  • ✝
    Faith-Centered
    Every project is approached as an act of stewardship — building with the gifts we've been given.
  • ◆
    Built to Last
    Honest materials & construction techniques that stand the test of time.
  • ▣
    Custom by Nature
    Each piece is designed around your specific space, needs, and story.
